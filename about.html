Originally from Pune, Maharashtra.

Hello, I'm Abhishek a multidisciplinary designer, visualizer and a developer hoping to futher my career in the Ui/Ux design space.

I was born in a small town in southern parts of Maharashtra and raised in Pune. I have completed my bacelors in commerce at Pune university, although being greatly infuenced by design and human psychology I decided to change my career path.

After completing my graduation I found myself not much interested in pursuing futher education in commerce and as being fond of people I landed my first job as customer relations associate at Amazon. My experience at Amazon thought me a lot about managing customer problems with in depth probing, empathy, altering custom resolutions for individuals and having customer/user centric approach while highly contributing to my communications and people skills.

However, with my inclianation towrds design I enrolled myself into a interior design and visualisation program and started working for Design Foundry - a architecture firm as a designer & visaulizer, my experience here taught me about team work, collabration, co-ordination and working under deadlines. Almost a year after I started working as a freelancer in design and visualisation.

Although, with evolving technology and a peep into the future, I developed facination for technology, design and human interaction with it and began my journey into Ui/Ux design.

Outside of design I love listening to music specially while cycling and very much like playing football.

I'm always down to colaborate, reach out and lets have a chat.
