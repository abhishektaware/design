Everything Pets

A 2 month long product design project for pet care & animal safety.

I pursued this as a personal project during my ui/ux course. It involved an extensive user research into existing products, various related individuals, proffesionals and organisations.

Team: 1 person.

OVERVIEW

Everything pets is an mobile application designed to provide a better living experience and timely pet care solutions for the user and his pet and also services by incorporating various professionals and organisations involved with pet industry while facilitating animal safety and awareness along with it.

ROLE

Product design, Product strategy, User research, Market research, Interaction design, Visual design, Material design, Information Architecture, Prototyping & Testing.

Understanding the problem

I conducted a few user interviews and brain storming sessions with my family members and friends who have one or multiple pets and came up with what if scenarios beforehand for my questionaires to probe them, and for a better understanding of their touch points.

The following was revealed:

  • Individuals struggled with the safety and healthcare issues regarding their pets .
  • Individuals often faced difficulty to keep a track of their pets hygiene & medical activity.
  • Individuals struggled finding pet friendly places, services and activities to spend more time with their pets.
  • Individuals were always worried about their pets safety and well being when away from home.
  • Individuals were confused about how and where to find the right professional help in emergencies.

Product vision and solution

From these findings, I decided as a product to identify the solutions and key business goals:

  • Enable the user to easily take care and track basic hygiene, grooming and medical care of their pets.
  • To provide an reach to the right professional help needed by the user and making it a easy and reliable experience.
  • Enable user to monitor and track their pets activity when away from them.
  • Help user find pet friendly places, services and exciting activities for the user and the pet to have an immersive experience together.
  • To enable user to reach out for help during emergencies by integrating a network of local NGO's and indviduals/ proffesionals as volunteers.

As a starting point I did some market research of the competition to investigate the current offferings in the market and took inspiration from the perticular features that I like about them.

Defining the MVP

Based on the research and the feature list defined, I started with lo-fi wireframing and derived at the following MVP features:

  • Create pet's profile.
    Users can create & setup their pets profiles.
  • schedule hygiene tasks and set a reminder.
    Users can setup what hygiene tasks they require for their pet like bath, nails , ears, grooming etc. and set reminder accordingly.
  • Input pets biological data.
    Users can record weight, tempreature and height data of their pets just to track their progress or for any medical referance.
  • Record medical history.
    Users can now easily keep track of their pets medical data such as vaccinations, diagnostic reports, specific events or any vetrinary visits and securely save and share it with family, friends or proffesionals when needed.

Designs

Although this being my first ui/ux project, I decided to defy the norms by using neumorphism in my material design.

Get started screen

This screen was simply created to showcase the application logo and encourage users to get started.

Login screen

Quick login using email or access using google, facebook or twitter account.

Users would fill in a simple sign up form to create a new account.

Users could also retrive password for their account.

Pet's profile

Setup your first pets profile by entering his details.

My Pets

All your pets listed with their basic info such as their name, birthday, breed and gender.

Hygiene tasks

Keep a track and set a reminder for your pets routine grooming and hygiene tasks like bath, nails, deworming, ear cleaning, brushing etc.

Manage tasks

You can easily postpone, prepone or delete any scheduled task at any time easily by just clicing the edit option next to the task.

Check upcoming, overdue and previous history of the selected task.

Health

Upload, record and share all your pet related medical and health documents with your veterinary, friends and family.

Set medicine and vaccine reminders.

Bio

Record your pets biological data like height, weight and tempreature for any referance in the future or, to track his timely growth.

Schedule

Look through the calendar for your pet related upcoming tasks, appointments and events.

Confirmation screen

Confirming a user action to double check with the user for any error or a second thought.

Success screen !

Affirmation to the user for any changes or additions done by them, have been successfully implemented.
